from django.conf import settings
from django.conf import global_settings



Full django.conf module structure:

django.conf itself is a package, and its commonly known members are:

settings – the main object for project settings.

settings.configure – method to configure settings manually (rarely used in scripts or tests).

global_settings – the default Django settings (used for reference).

urls – django.conf.urls (actually a submodule, not directly imported from django.conf).
